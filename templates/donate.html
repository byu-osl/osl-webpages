Help Fund Us

The BYU Open Source Lab relies on generous gifts from community members to support our work. Donations go directly to fund student internships in our lab, as we mentor their work on one of our projects. Contributions made directly to the university are tax deductible.

The support you give will help students work on the active projects shown on our projects page. If you have an interest in a particular project, please contact Daniel Zappala, the director of the lab, at daniel.zappala@gmail.com.

If you are interested in donating, please visit the BYU Giving site. Be sure to specify Computer Science Scholarships and Mentorships in the selection area and specify BYU Open Source Lab in the comments area.
